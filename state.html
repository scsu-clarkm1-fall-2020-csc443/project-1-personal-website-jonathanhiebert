State and Local Elections Are Important Too!

As important as it is to use your right to vote for our next U.S. President, voting in state and local elections is important in its own right. The outcomes of these elections directly impact the day-to-day lives of U.S. citizens.

In order to make sustainable, sufficient change, voter turnout at state and local elections needs to be on par with that of presidential elections. If you would like learn more about state and local elections, check out the links below!
